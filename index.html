What is Breaking
(Break Dancing)?

70's - 80's

Breaking is a style of dance that originated in the United States in the 1970s.

By the 1980s, breaking reached a greater audience when it was adopted by mainstream artists such as Michael Jackson.

Breaking had gone from a street phenomenon to one that was embraced by the wider culture.
It is around this time that the term break dancing was invented by the media.

90's - 00's

Through out the 90’s and 00’s,
Breaking kept growing as an underground dance and developed it’s own sub-culture.

International competitions such as
FREESTYLE SESSION, BOTY, IBE and
REDBULL BCONE are a few examples of how breaking as a culture has constantly been developing.

10's - 20's

Breaking made its Olympic debut at the Youth Olympic Games in Buenos Aires in 2018.

Following its outstanding success, breaking has been chosen to feature on the Paris 2024 Olympic sports program as a new sport, along with surfing, skateboarding and sport climbing.
